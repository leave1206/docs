`kimi-k2-6` is Moonshot's long-context flagship — strong on Chinese tasks with a **1M-token context window**, second only to Gemini 2.5 Pro on ByteSpike. On ByteSpike, it is served through the **Volcengine CodePlan subscription channel**.

## Channels

| Channel | Provider | Base URL | Billing | Latency p50 | Region |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Volcengine CodePlan** | Volcengine subscription | `ark.cn-beijing.volces.com/api/coding/v3` | Subscription (monthly, admin-amortized) | ~600ms | CN |

## Protocols

| Protocol | Path |
|---|---|
| OpenAI Chat Completions | `POST https://llm.bytespike.ai/v1/chat/completions` |

## Quickstart

```bash
curl https://llm.bytespike.ai/v1/chat/completions \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $BYTESPIKE_API_KEY" \
  -H "content-type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "model": "kimi-k2-6",
    "messages": [{ "role": "user", "content": "Hello, ByteSpike." }]
  }'
```

- **Long-document Chinese-language QA** — 1M context for codebase-scale or full-document review.
- **CN-region multi-doc synthesis** — Kimi's strongest dimension.
- **Cost-predictable long-context** — without per-token billing, you can throw 500K-token prompts at it without watching the meter.
