`doubao-seed-2.0-pro` is the flagship of ByteDance's Doubao family — strong on Chinese-language tasks, vision-capable, with a long context window. On ByteSpike, it is served through the **Volcengine CodePlan subscription channel**, sharing one credential with DeepSeek-V3.2, GLM, Kimi, and MiniMax.

## Channels

| Channel | Provider | Base URL | Billing | Latency p50 | Region |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Volcengine CodePlan** | Volcengine subscription | `ark.cn-beijing.volces.com/api/coding/v3` | Subscription (monthly, admin-amortized) | ~500ms | CN |

Per-token cost on the request line item is zero; the cost sits at the subscription level. See [/providers/volcengine-codeplan](/providers/volcengine-codeplan) for the amortization model.

## Protocols

| Protocol | Path |
|---|---|
| OpenAI Chat Completions | `POST https://llm.bytespike.ai/v1/chat/completions` |

## Quickstart

```bash
curl https://llm.bytespike.ai/v1/chat/completions \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $BYTESPIKE_API_KEY" \
  -H "content-type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "model": "doubao-seed-2.0-pro",
    "messages": [{ "role": "user", "content": "Hello, ByteSpike." }]
  }'
```

## When to use

- **Chinese-language workloads** — Doubao's strongest dimension.
- **Mixed-LLM teams** — the CodePlan subscription bundles five families; if your team uses several, this channel pays for itself.
- **Vision tasks in CN region** — strong OCR + image understanding.
